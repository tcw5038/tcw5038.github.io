JobReady

This app helps users keep track of their progress with their job applications. Users can add new job applications, keep track of where they are in the process, edit these applications as they progress, delete, and filter them.

Urban Adventures

This app functions like a digital record of a user's urban travel experiences. Users can record cities that they have traveled to with images, tags, and summaries. Additionally, using Google Maps, users are able to see pins of their cities visually on the map.

GameFinder

This project utilizes the Giant Bomb and Twitch APIs to help users find video games that they are interested in. Users can use search or simply click on a popular game to get results for related games that they might be interested in. They can then click the related game to get more information.
